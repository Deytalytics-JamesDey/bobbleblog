Hello to Bobble

Date Sun 06 September 2020 By James Dey Category Puppy. Tags bolognese puppies

Hello Bobble. Bobble's an 8 week old Bolognese) puppy, which are part of the Bichon family alongside Bichon Frise, Maltese, Havanese & Coton de Tulear. The Bolognese differs from the other Bichons in that they have just 1 layer of silky wool which looks and feels like cotton wool.

I wanted to call him Spag after Charlie & I's favourite food, but the name is because he looks like a bobble on a bobble hat.

His mum is called Lulu and his dad is Frank. He also has 1 brother and 2 sisters who he used to love fighting with. He's very quiet most of the time but was quite loud when we first put him in the car.

He seems to have settled down very quickly and has inherited his mum's ability to stand on 2 legs to beg for a treat.
